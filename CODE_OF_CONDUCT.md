# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and empathetic.
- Assume positive intent and communicate clearly.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Prioritize student safety, privacy, and ethical AI principles in discussions and contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional, inclusive environment.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private GitHub issue addressed to the maintainer, or contact the project owner directly through GitHub.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with care and confidentiality.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.
